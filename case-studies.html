How We Scaled a DTC Skincare Brand from $18K to $92K/Month in 90 Days

Client Bloom Skincare Co.
Industry DTC E-Commerce
Timeline 90 Days
Services Meta Ads, Landing Pages, Email
0
Revenue Growth
0

0

The Challenge

Bloom Skincare had a loyal Instagram following and a quality product line, but couldn't profitably scale beyond $18K/month in revenue. Two previous agencies had burned through $40K in ad spend with diminishing returns, broken tracking, and no clear attribution model. The founder was ready to pull all paid advertising.

Our Strategy

Rather than throwing more budget at broken campaigns, we rebuilt from the ground up:

  • 1. Rebuilt conversion tracking with server-side events for accurate attribution
  • 2. Designed 5 new landing pages with focused messaging for each product category
  • 3. Created a tiered Meta ad structure: cold → warm → retarget with unique creative per tier
  • 4. Implemented a 6-email post-purchase sequence to drive repeat orders
  • 5. Launched a referral program that turned customers into acquisition channels

Timeline of Results

Days 1–30

$18K → $34K/mo

Fixed tracking, launched new creatives

Days 31–60

$34K → $61K/mo

Scaled winning ads, launched email flows

Days 61–90

$61K → $92K/mo

Expanded to Google, repeat purchases kicked in

We'd burned $40K with two other agencies. Apex rebuilt our tracking, launched clean campaigns, and generated more revenue in 30 days than the previous agencies did in 6 months combined.

The Challenge

VaultEdge had a strong product but relied entirely on founder-led sales and referrals. Their website converted at under 1%, their LinkedIn presence was non-existent, and they had no systematic way to generate and nurture enterprise leads. The CEO needed a predictable pipeline before their Series B raise.

Our Strategy

  • 1. Redesigned the website with conversion-focused architecture and clear ICPs
  • 2. Built a lead magnet (industry report) and gated content funnel
  • 3. Launched targeted LinkedIn ad campaigns to decision-makers at ideal accounts
  • 4. Created a 5-touch email nurture sequence to move MQLs to sales calls
  • 5. Published 3 thought leadership pieces per month for organic authority

Apex Growth gave us a repeatable, predictable pipeline for the first time in our company's history. That pipeline was instrumental in closing our Series B round.
